What we do

Sensory panels tell you something tastes bitter, or that the heat is too sharp, or that your reformulation lost something. They can't tell you why at the molecular level.

We profile products against human taste and chemesthetic receptors to identify exactly which biological targets are activated, how strongly, and what's driving it. This turns subjective sensory observations into actionable molecular data.
